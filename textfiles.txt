eva zornio:

After first gaining a degree in Biology and a master's degree in neuroscience, Eva Zornio's focus is in the field of arts. Circulating between institutions and independent structures, she performed at the Swiss Institute in Rome (2019). She exhibited at the Fondation d'Entreprise Ricard (2019), at Kunsthaus Langenthal (2019), and at Forde (2018). In 2020 she participated in the exhibition of the Bourses de la Ville de Genève at the Centre d'Art Contemporain and she was nominated for the Swiss Art Awards 2021. In 2022 she exhibited at Kunst Halle Sankt Gallen and was selected for the 2023 publication of the Cahiers d'Artistes of Pro Helvetia.

viola poli


Viola Poli (*1992) est une artiste originaire du Tessin qui vit et travaille à Genève. Elle est diplômée Bachelor en 2019 et Master en 2021 de la HEAD–Genève, en passant par la HGB Leipzig (DE). Muta est sa deuxième exposition personnelle après Tutto quello che (en collaboration avec Elias Njima et Léna Sophia Bagutti), Espace Libre, Bienne en 2022.
Elle a participé à plusieurs expositions comme Bonne Brise, TOPIC, Genève et GO GO GO, Le Grütli, Genève en 2022, La Regionale, Villa Ciani, Lugano, Art au Centre, Halle Nord, Genève et terrains vagues, Limbo, Genève en 2021, Space Invasion, Galerie Fabienne Levy, Lausanne en 2020, Domestic Plants, oqbo Gallery, Berlin (DE), Fête de la grève féministe, Arsenic, Lausanne, et Insonne, avec Sonnenstube, Spazio Morel, Lugano en 2019, Antipodes, On Arte Gallery, Minusio et Soucoupes volantes, Kunsthaus Langenthal en 2018.
Elle a participé à la résidence PICTO, Genève en 2021-2022 et Bally Artist, Festival du Film de Locarno en 2021.

Viola Poli (*1992) is an artist from Ticino who lives and works in Geneva. She graduated with a Bachelors degree in 2019 and a Masters degree in 2021 from HEAD–Geneva. Muta is her second solo show after Tutto quello che (in collaboration with Elias Njima et Léna Sophia Bagutti), Espace Libre, Bienne in 2022.
She has participated in several exhibitions such as Bonne Brise, TOPIC, Geneva and GO GO GO, Le Grütli, Geneva in 2022, La Regionale, Villa Ciani, Lugano, Art au Centre, Halle Nord, Geneva and terrains vagues, Limbo, Geneva in 2021, Space Invasion, Galerie Fabienne Levy, Lausanne in 2020, Domestic Plants, oqbo Gallery, Berlin (DE), Feminist Strike Festival, Arsenic, Lausanne, and Insonne, with Sonnenstube, Spazio Morel, Lugano in 2019, Antipodes, On Arte Gallery, Minusio and Flying Saucers, Kunsthaus Langenthal in 2018.
She participated in the PICTO residency, Geneva in 2021-2022 and Bally Artist, Locarno Film Festival in 2021.
